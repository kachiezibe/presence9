Who I am
Kachi Ezibe — Founder

I'm Kachi — a 17-year-old entrepreneur building AI automation systems and digital products from the ground up. I started with a simple observation: small businesses lose thousands every month not because they're bad at what they do, but because their systems are broken.

My uncle's restaurant was losing customers because people couldn't find him online. I built him a website in two weeks. His bookings doubled. That was the beginning.

Now I run two companies. One builds websites and digital presence for small businesses. The other builds AI automation systems for real estate brokerages — systems that respond to leads in seconds, qualify prospects automatically, and close deals while agents sleep.

I'm not interested in building one thing. I'm interested in building systems that compound.

2023

The First Website

Built my uncle's restaurant website in two weeks. His bookings doubled. Realised small businesses don't have tech problems — they have business problems that technology can solve. PresenceIX was born.

2024

Going Deeper Into AI

Started studying automation — Make.com, n8n, AI APIs. Built my first real estate lead follow-up system. Watched a brokerage go from losing 40% of their leads to responding to every single one in under 5 seconds.

2024

PresenceIX Repositioned

Pivoted the agency from general web design to AI automation exclusively for real estate brokerages. The niche is clear, the problem is expensive, and the ROI is undeniable. Retainers start at $2,000/month.

2025

CallSaver AI Conceptualised

Identified a second market: HVAC companies in Dallas losing $150–300K per year to missed calls. Built the architecture for an AI that responds to missed calls in 60 seconds, qualifies leads, generates quotes, and books appointments automatically.

2026

Folio Studio Launched

Spun out the web design work into its own brand. Folio Studio handles websites, branding, and digital presence for small businesses at transparent pricing. Two companies. Two markets. One operator.
