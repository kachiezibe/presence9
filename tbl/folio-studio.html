FAQ

Questions
answered.

01

How long does a website take?

Most projects land in 2–4 weeks from discovery call to launch. Larger builds can go to 6 weeks. The timeline is always agreed upfront — no ambiguity.

02

Do I need to know anything technical?

Not at all. I handle everything from design to development to launch. You tell me what your business does and who you're selling to. I handle the rest.

03

Will I own my website?

100%. Once final payment is made, everything is yours — domain, hosting, code. No monthly fees to me, no lock-ins. Full ownership, full control.

04

Can I update the site myself?

Yes. If you want a CMS (like WordPress or Webflow), we build it in. I also provide a training walkthrough so you can update content independently.

05

What happens after launch?

Every package includes 30 days of free support. After that, monthly maintenance ($50–150/month) or pay-as-you-go. Nothing forced — you choose what works.

06

How much does it cost?

Starter sites from $500. Professional from $1,200. Custom builds from $2,000. All prices are fixed and published in the pricing section above. No surprises.
